Team

Teams are collections of players. In many gametypes, scores and victory conditions are calculated at the team level.

Actions

set_score
Reach Halo 4 H2A

Sets or adjusts the current score for the entire team.

Syntax: team.set_score(number)

RVT Equivalent: team.score

get_scoreboard_pos
Reach Halo 4 H2A

Returns the rank of this team relative to others on the scoreboard.

RVT Equivalent: team.get_scoreboard_pos

set_co_op_spawning
Reach

Enables or disables buddy-based spawning for all members of the team (Reach exclusive).

RVT Equivalent: team.set_co_op_spawning

Member Conditions

has_any_players
Reach Halo 4 H2A

Returns true if the team contains at least one player.

RVT Equivalent: team.has_any_players
