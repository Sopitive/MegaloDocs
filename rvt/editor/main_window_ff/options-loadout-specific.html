Loadout Tier

A loadout tier is a group of loadouts that players may be able to access depending on the gametype script. Each loadout tier has five loadouts, which can be enabled and disabled, and configured.

The user interface shows the contents of one loadout at a time. To edit any given loadout, click its name.

The fields available to edit for each loadout are as follows:

Visible In-Game
Controls whether players can see and select this loadout during a match.
Name
Controls the loadout's name.
Primary Weapon
Controls what Primary Weapon players spawn with when they have chosen this loadout.
Secondary Weapon
Controls what Secondary Weapon players spawn with when they have chosen this loadout.
Armor Ability
Controls what Armor Ability players spawn with when they have chosen this loadout.
Grenade Count
Controls what grenades players spawn with when they have chosen this loadout.
